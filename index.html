With many Educational Institutions facing physical campus closures and moving to online learning due to COVID-19 , Adobe in partnership with Atal Innovation Mission, Niti Aayog is happy to introduce a series of Webinars for teachers across Atal Tinkering Labs. The main objective of this webinar is to help teachers learn Adobe Spark to foster Digital Literacy and Creativity Skills of the students.
